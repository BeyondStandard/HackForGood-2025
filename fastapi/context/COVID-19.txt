COVID-19

What service?: On the website of RIVM (the National Institute for Public Health and Environment) you find the current numbers of corona cases in the Netherlands.
Link to Website: https://www.rivm.nl/en
What do you need to know?: Request an appointment for advice by phone, whatsapp or email. 
All advice is now given at distance (office closed) due to the corona crisis.
No documents are needed.
