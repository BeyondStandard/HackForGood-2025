First Asylum Request

Offer Name: Request asylum at Ter Apel (Groningen)
What service?: To request asylum for the Netherlands go to Aanmeldcentrum Ter Apel. You can get a free train ticket to go to Ter Apel at the Humanitarian Service Point (HSP) in the Red Cross office in Amsterdam at the Valkenburgerstraat 24. The HSP is opened from monday to friday from 12.00 - 20.00pm.
Address: Ter Apelervenen 3
