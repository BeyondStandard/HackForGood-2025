Emergency Lines

Offer Name: In case of emergency
What service?: For life-threatening issues, call 112. Everyone can call 112 for free, also if you are undocumented!
Phone Number: 112.0
Opening Hours Weekdays: Available 24/7
Opening Hours Weekends: Available 24/7
What do you need to know?: Call 112 immediately in life threatening situations! For example in case of:
• fire in your house
• urgent safety issues
• a bad accident or injury
• a heart attack
• other health emergencies or life threatening situations that require immediate care.

**If you are at risk or victim of a crime, you can safely call the police. Even when you are undocumented.**
