Mental Wellbeing

Offer Name: Video about stress
What service?: The Red Cross created a video about how people can cope with stress. You can access the video here. 
Link to Website: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dl_p6PAOA2o 
What do you need to know?: Many people that live undocumented in the Netherlands have a lot of stress due to their situation. This video gives more information about what stress is, how to recognize it and how you can deal with it in a healthy way. It offers some advice that could be of help for people under a lot of stress.
